泰勒公式的推导过程

设函数 \(x_0 = 0\) 即 \(x\) 在 \(0\) 处有不限的导数，则

\[\begin{align} \large {f(x)} &\large{= a_0 (x-x_0)^0 + a_1 (x-x_0)^1 + a_2 (x-x_0)^2 + \cdots + a_n (x-x_0)^n + \cdots} \\ \\ &\large{= a_0 + a_1 x + a_2 x^2 + a_3 x^3 + \cdots + a_n x^n + \cdots} \end{align}\]

我们可以轻易知道：

\[\begin{align} \large{ f(0)} & \large{= a_0 = 0! a_0} \\ \large{f'(0)} & \large{= a_1 = 1! a_1} \\ \large{f''(0)} & \large{= 2 a_2 = 2! a_2} \\ \large{f'''(0)} & \large{= 6 a_3 = 3! a_3} \\ \vdots \\ \vdots \\ f^{(n)}(0) & \large{= n! a_n} \end{align}\]

故：

\(\large {a_n = \dfrac {f^{(n)}(0)} {n !} }\)

从而：

\(\large{f(x) = \sum_{i=0}^n a_i \, x_i + R(x)} \,\,\,\, (R(x) 为余项)\) \(\\\) \(\large{其中：}\) \(\\\) \(\large{a_i = \dfrac {f^{(i)} (0)} {i !}}\)
